NYCValues is a collaboration designed and produced entirely by graduate students from NYU’s graduate Studio 20 program. Inspired by Ted Cruz’s claim that Donald Trump has “New York values,” 13 students set out to discover what makes the city unique. But don’t take our word for it. Look into the lives of 6 different New Yorkers and vote for the values you think the represent.
